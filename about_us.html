Our Mission

AI FOR ALL is a student-led organization at UCI dedicated to introducing, educating, and empowering students of all majors to explore the world of Artificial Intelligence. Our mission is to break barriers and provide access to AI knowledge for students from diverse academic backgroundsâ€”whether technical or non-technical. Through interactive workshops, events, mentorship programs, and publications, we aim to show that AI is not just a field for ICS students but a tool that has applications across every industry.

What is AI FOR ALL?

AI FOR ALL is designed to make AI education accessible, practical, and exciting for all students at UCI. We believe that every major, from business to biology, and from psychology to the arts, can benefit from AI. Our club fosters a collaborative environment where students gain foundational AI knowledge, build real-world skills, and explore innovative AI applications in their areas of interest.

Key Committees and Programs

1. Build on AI Committee

Focus: Mentorship and AI Projects for ICS Freshmen/Minors

  • Designed to help ICS freshmen dive into AI through hands-on projects.
  • Mentees collaborate with peers and receive guidance from experienced ICS mentors.
  • Students learn AI fundamentals, participate in workshops, and showcase their projects in spring.

2. AI Explorers Committee

Focus: Interactive Workshops and Events for Non-ICS Majors

  • Designed primarily for non-ICS majors to introduce AI basics and its applications.
  • Regular workshops focus on beginner-friendly coding, real-world AI use cases, and interdisciplinary AI applications.
  • Specialized events tailored to specific fields (e.g., healthcare, marketing, and arts) help students see AI's relevance in their industries.

3. AI Digest

Focus: Research and Publication

  • AI Digest provides valuable insights into AI at UCI and beyond.
  • Content includes:
    • AI-related research and publications at UCI.
    • Professor interviews and student/alumni contributions to the field of AI.
    • Campus involvement and global innovations in AI.
  • The Digest keeps students informed, inspired, and connected to the world of AI.

4. OMNI AI Committee

Focus: AI Workshops for Everyone

  • OMNI AI offers simplified, hands-on workshops for students of all levels.
  • Workshops cover topics like AI basics, beginner coding, and real-world applications across fields.
  • Participants build mini-projects, engage in interactive sessions, and apply AI knowledge to solve challenges in creative ways.
