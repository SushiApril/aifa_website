AI Explorers

Empowering Students Across All Majors
to Discover the World of AI

Our Mission

The AI Explorers Committee is dedicated to introducing Artificial Intelligence to students of all majors, with a special focus on Non-ICS students. Our mission is to make AI accessible, inclusive, and engaging for everyoneâ€”whether you are studying business, biology, art, or engineering.

We aim to:

  • Demystify AI by providing foundational knowledge through hands-on workshops and specialized events.
  • Help students understand AI applications in their respective fields of study.
  • Foster a welcoming environment for curiosity-driven exploration and collaboration.
  • Equip participants with practical skills that make them AI-ready for any industry.

What do we do?

AI is transforming industries worldwide, and understanding its potential is key to staying ahead. AI Explorers bridges the gap for students who are eager to learn but may not come from a computer science background.

Explore AI Basics

Learn about computing, machine learning, and AI concepts in a beginner-friendly manner.

Specialized Learning

Discover how AI is applied in fields like business, healthcare, economics, social sciences, and more.

Practical Coding

Gain hands-on experience with easy-to-follow coding workshops.

Collaborative Events

Participate in specialized events and projects tailored to students' interests.

Problem-Solving Skills

Learn to apply AI concepts to solve tasks relevant to your area of study.

Whether you're new to AI or curious about its impact on your field, AI Explorers ensures you're equipped with the tools and knowledge to grow.

Workshops Include

AI Fundamentals

Basics of AI, machine learning, and its real-world applications.

Coding for AI Beginners

Learn Python and simple AI tools, no coding experience needed.

Field-Specific AI Applications

Discover AI's role in business analytics, healthcare predictions, art generation, and more.

Project-Based Learning

Hands-on tasks that help you understand AI concepts through interactive challenges.

Team Collaboration

Work with peers to brainstorm and solve problems using AI tools.
