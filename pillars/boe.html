Build on AI

A flagship initiative under UCI's AI FOR ALL Club
Empowering ICS freshmen and minors through hands-on AI learning

Our Mission

The Build on AI Committee is committed to fostering an inclusive and collaborative learning environment for ICS freshmen and minors. Our mission is to introduce Artificial Intelligence concepts in a structured, practical, and hands-on manner, empowering students to create impactful projects and gain the skills needed to succeed in this transformative field.

We aim to:

  • Equip mentees with the technical foundation and critical problem-solving skills needed to thrive in AI.
  • Bridge the knowledge gap between beginners and advanced AI learners through mentorship and collaboration.
  • Inspire curiosity and creativity in AI applications that address real-world challenges across industries.
  • Cultivate a strong AI learning community at UCI where students support, teach, and grow together.
